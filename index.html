About 3D Recruit Education

3D Recruit Education is a specialist recruitment agency for Primary and Secondary School teaching jobs.  Located in Battle, near Hastings we are ideally situated to provide local teaching professionals to the East Sussex and Kent region. 

We see our size as one of our biggest strengths as we are able to deliver a personalised, reactive service to all of our candidates and clients.  3D Recruit Education is one of the few Compliance Plus accredited agencies, and our staff are some of the most experienced within the field of recruitment.  We are members of APSCo, a governing body for the recruitment industry and adhere to their standards.  

3D Recruit Education offer supply and permanent positions in the following areas: 

  • Primary School Teaching
  • Secondary School Teaching
  • Head Teachers and Assistant Head Teachers
  • Consultant Head Teachers
  • Education Consultants
  • Education Advisors
  • College Tutors
  • Invigilators
  • Teaching Assistants
  • Specialist Teaching Assistants
  • SENCOs
  • Pupil Referral Units
